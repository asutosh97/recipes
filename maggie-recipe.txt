Ingredients:
 -1 pkt 2 minute maggie noodles
 -maggie masala
 -1 teaspoon of salt
 -1 cap of oil
 -maggie's hot and sweet tomato sauce
 -mustard seeds

Directions:
 1.Put a container on gas with some oil on it.Warm it.

 2.Add some water and let it boil.

 3.Then add the maggie packet and its masala.Steer the mixture 
 properly.

 4.Add tomato sauce to the mixture and then again steer it
 well.Decorate and serve.
